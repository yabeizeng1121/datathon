Problem Statement

Identifying the Gap between K-12 Technology Talent and Recruitment Pipeline.

1. A Gap Between Job Availability and Candidate Suitability:
The data proved that 92.2% fewer entry-level tech jobs
compared to senior-level positions in the recent market.
Furthermore, research from LinkedIn.com reveals that 41%
of recruiters find entry-level roles the most challenging
to recruit for due to a lack of candidates with the required skills.



2. A Gap Between Academic Performance in Early Education:
Data shows a sharp 51.8% drop in students' math and science
scores from 5th grade to 6th grade, indicating that students
start falling behind in key STEM areas early on.



3. A Gap Between Student Achievements and Recruitment Expectations:
The 2023 CCR Benchmark Report reveals that while career readiness
typically starts in 8th grade across the nation, by then, few students
have the strong math and science scores needed to qualify for CTE programs
or enter the tech job market successfully.
